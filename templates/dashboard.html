Project Overview

This project leverages edge computing and computer vision for proactive wildfire management. By integrating a camera with a Raspberry Pi running an optimized deep learning model, the system detects early signs of fire and smoke in real-time. This on-device analysis drastically reduces latency, enabling quicker alert notifications than traditional methods and aiming to minimize the spread and damage caused by forest fires.
